About Us

We, the students of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DCSE) of College of Engineering Guindy (CEG), the main campus of Anna University, are thrilled to be associated with PSNA College of Engineering and Technology to host our annual inter collegiate outreach program, Reach. Every year, Reach is conducted to create a inter college platform for students to learn of cutting edge technology and trending topics in the Industry by creating a hands-on learning environment for students to interact with both peers and professionals.

We have planned to visit your college on December 22nd and 23rd. Various colleges from around the city will also be called for the event. We have made plans for 3 workshops and a placement guidance session as well. All the workshops that have been planned will have two sessions. As per the current plan, one session of each of the workshops will be conducted per day. Please find the details of the workshops below,

Python

This workshop aims to introduce Python as a multifunctional productive language and talk of the growing demands for the same in the computer industry. Widely used frameworks will be discussed and hands-on sessions will also be conducted for a more practical and fulfilling experience for the participating students

Design Workshop

The design workshop includes demonstration of robust softwares for editing images and videos. New trends in UI / UX design will also be discussed during this workshop. The first session will be an interactive session while the latter will be a hands-on session featuring educational demos.

Machine Learning and Deep Learning

As a new era of computing dawns upon us, we introduce and dive deeper into Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technologies. A hands-on training session using simple datasets will be held to encourage students to gear themselves for a future in Machine Learning.

Placement Talks and Mentoring

Through this workshop, we hope to share our experiences on handling placement preparations and the key to cracking tough interviews. Both sessions will present a concise database of essential material available online and will provide tips and tricks to help you get one step closer to your dreams.
